How Platform Payouts Work — Understanding the Numbers

CUSTOMER PAYS (Gross Sales)
Item Subtotal + Packaging
− Restaurant Promo/Discount
+ GST (5% collected for Govt.)
= Grand Total charged to customer
PLATFORM DEDUCTS (Before Paying You)
Platform Commission (~10%)
+ Other Fees (payment gateway, Govt. charges)
= Total Deductions
YOU RECEIVE (Weekly Bank Transfer)
Grand Total − Platform Deductions
= Net Payout (what hits your FED BANK / YES BANK weekly)
⚠️ Note on your Reconciliation table: The "App Gross" column shows what customers paid (Grand Total). The "Ledger" column is your 75% estimate. But what actually reaches your bank is Net Payout (Swiggy ~80.4%, Zomato ~77.1%). This Payout Analytics page tracks Net Payout accurately.
